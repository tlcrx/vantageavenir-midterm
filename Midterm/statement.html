Design Statement

The VantageAvenir project captures the fusion of storytelling, heritage, and travel design. This website explores clarity, usability, and emotional resonance.

Built with Flexbox, the design ensures adaptive layouts that remain cohesive across screens. Each page maintains consistency in structure and accessibility—key principles of good UX.

The goal was to balance elegant simplicity with narrative warmth, reflecting both modern web design principles and the authentic culture of Martinique.

Design Approach

Typography

Combines Montserrat for structure and Lora for warmth, emphasizing contrast and readability.

Color Palette

Uses celadon greens, warm accents, and soft neutrals to evoke tropical serenity and cultural vibrancy.

Layout & Accessibility

Built with responsive Flexbox containers and Grid layouts to ensure intuitive navigation and clear visual hierarchy.
