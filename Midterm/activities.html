Top Activities & Attractions

Martinique offers a wealth of experiences—from rainforest adventures to cultural treasures. Here are a few highlights to make your visit unforgettable.

Hike Mount Pelée

Challenge yourself on this breathtaking volcano trail with panoramic views of the island and sea.

Visit Jardin de Balata

Discover over 3,000 tropical plant species in this lush botanical garden outside Fort-de-France.

Relax at Anse Noire

Swim in the crystal-clear waters of Martinique’s only black-sand beach, perfect for snorkeling.

Rhum Distillery Tours

Explore the island’s renowned rhum agricole distilleries and sample locally crafted blends.
